Three best movies in telugu according to Narayana

My top three movies of all time

Rangasthalam

This movie was directed by one of the best directors of Tollywood Film industry Sukumar. The movie is about a deaf guy who is so kind hearted and had a beautiful family and a brother. This movie revolves around the politis in a village, which will lead the movie to thriller and emotional ride of the hero who is so dear to brother, who was killed by someone during this politics in the village.

RRR

This movie was directed by S.S.Rajmouli, who had got Oscar for the song named Naatu Naatu for the film he directed.This is movie of two Indian freedom fighters who made a revolution against British Government during the british colonisation in India
